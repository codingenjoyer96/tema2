Introduction to Pupin Coils

Pupin's 1899 patent for loading coils, archaically called "Pupin coils", was a groundbreaking advancement in telecommunications.

Historical Background

The technology followed closely on the pioneering work of the English physicist and mathematician Oliver Heaviside, which predates Pupin's patent by some seven years.

Patent Acquisition and Impact

The importance of the patent was recognized when the American rights to it were acquired by American Telephone & Telegraph (AT&T), making Pupin wealthy. Although AT&T bought Pupin's patent, they made little use of it as they had their own development led by George Campbell and had up to this point been challenging Pupin with Campbell's own patent.

AT&T feared losing control of an invention which was immensely valuable due to its ability to greatly extend the range of long-distance telephones and especially submarine ones.
